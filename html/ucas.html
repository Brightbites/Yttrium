    To apply to most universitys in the United Kingdom you must apply through UCAS

    Before even applying on the UCAS website you must first make an account at accounts.ucas.com/account/login

    once you press the "Register" button you will be in the onboarding process (this is just for UCAS to give recomendations of uni and coursces)

    after the onboarding process is finished you will be on this page

    when you are ready to start your application press the "Start an application" button

    you will need a Buzzword from your college or high school so UCAS can request your exam results

    once you have filled in the sections you are ready to submit your application with a personal statement
